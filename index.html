About

A home should be a personal sanctuary.

Samal_Studio is a full-service residential design studio for clients who value thoughtful detail, calm luxury and a home with lasting character.

We combine custom elements, tactile materials and practical planning, collaborating closely with homeowners, architects, contractors and artisans from concept through completion.

Studio Philosophy

Each project is shaped around the client’s routine, taste and aspirations. The goal is not trend, but a space that feels deeply natural to return to.
